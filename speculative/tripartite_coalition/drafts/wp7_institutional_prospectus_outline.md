# Working Paper 7: Institutional Investment Prospectus (Outline)

## Module 1: Capitalization Table
*   **First-Loss Catalytic Equity**: 40% capitalization slice. Sourced via Program-Related Investments (PRIs) from philanthropic endowments. Subordinates return to protect senior debt.
*   **Senior Concessionary Debt**: 60% capitalization slice. Sourced via Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFIs). Secure, stabilized yield target.
*   **Acquisition Metric**: Maximum $150 per square foot entry price targeting Class B/C distressed commercial real estate.

## Module 2: CalAIM Revenue Floors
*   **Phase Zero Activation**: Housing Transition Navigation Services (H0043). Underwrites street-level trust accumulation.
*   **Physical Securitization**: Housing Deposits (T2038 U1). Finances initial capital transition.
*   **Continuous Co-Regulation**: Housing Tenancy and Sustaining Services (T2038). Finances rolling 24-month relational continuity.
*   **Acute Metabolic Stabilization**: Recuperative Care / Medical Respite (H2036). Isolates severe physiological volatility from standard housing pool.

## Module 3: Occupancy Modeling
*   **Month 0-6 (Ramp-up)**: 30% occupancy threshold. Prioritizes high CIWA score activations via Recuperative Care channels.
*   **Month 6-18 (Stabilization)**: 75% occupancy threshold. Transitions billing toward standard Tenancy and Sustaining Services.
*   **Month 18-36 (Maturity)**: 95% occupancy threshold. 8.0% Internal Rate of Return (IRR) stabilizes and distributes.

## Module 4: Downside Protection & Governance
*   **Asset Insulation**: Delaware Statutory Trust (DST) holds physical property deed.
*   **Operational Mandate**: California Public Benefit Corporation (PBC) executes relational street protocols.
*   **Catastrophic Lease Reassignment**: Master Lease triggers automatic operational transfer upon PBC insolvency. Preserves real estate asset from liquidation while protecting senior debt holders.
