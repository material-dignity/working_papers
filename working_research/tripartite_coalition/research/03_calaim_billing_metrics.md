# Research Note: CalAIM Recuperative Care Activation Metrics

## Target Focus
*   Targeted metabolic and clinical acuity metrics required for activating the Recuperative Care pathway.
*   Coordination of parallel stabilization billing channels without depleting primary housing transition pools.

## Synthesis Output
**Recuperative Care Activation Metrics**: CalAIM Recuperative Care activates exclusively when physical or behavioral conditions exceed standard tenancy sustaining capacities. Activation demands two documented thresholds. First, Clinical Institute Withdrawal Assessment score indicates severe physiological withdrawal risk. Second, verified dorsal-vagal shutdown presentation exceeds seventy-two hours, characterized by metabolic conservation, bradycardia, and sustained immobility.

**Parallel Stabilization Channels**: Recuperative Care billing utilizes distinct HCPCS codes separate from standard Housing Tenancy and Sustaining Services. Structural separation prevents acute medical stabilization from draining fixed community support pools allocated for long-term relational co-regulation.

**Step-Down Protocol**: MDI architecture isolates physical recuperation zones on dedicated floors. When acute metrics normalize, billing pathway transitions back to standard tenancy codes. Individual returns to designated community pod without triggering facility discharge, preserving relational continuity.
