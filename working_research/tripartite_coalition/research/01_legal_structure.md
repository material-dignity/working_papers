# Research Note: Legal Structure and Jurisdictional Framework

## Target Focus
*   The state jurisdiction for incorporating the Stewardship Trust.
*   The state jurisdiction for incorporating the Public Benefit Corporation (PBC).
*   Charter mechanisms preventing asset liquidation and insulating equity from operational liabilities.

## Synthesis Output
**Stewardship Trust Jurisdiction**: Delaware Statutory Trust (DST). Delaware law provides absolute statutory asset shielding, preventing creditors of operating entities from reaching trust assets. Trust formation requires zero annual state franchise tax filings. Private governance agreements explicitly mandate property retention for specified public benefit, locking commercial real estate out of speculative liquidation.

**Public Benefit Corporation Jurisdiction**: California. Incorporating operating entity domestically aligns structural governance with California Attorney General oversight regarding charitable asset usage. California PBC charter must embed Relational Dignity standard as specific public benefit purpose, legally subordinating dividend maximization to street-level metric delivery.

**Governance Firewall**: Master Lease Agreement between DST (Owner) and PBC (Operator) establishes absolute legal firewall. Agreement mandates that any operational bankruptcy or catastrophic liability event within PBC automatically triggers lease reassignment clause. Mechanism preserves physical asset inside DST for next designated operator while protecting capital investors from operational ruin.
